Johnson College Prep

Our Mission

At Johnson College Prep, our scholars are introduced to rigorous content and real world experiences that will prepare them for life after graduation. Our mission is to prepare ALL scholars for post secondary success while developing their social awareness and passion to serve the communities of Chicago.

Student Expectations

  1. Be present and on time every day with all required materials.
  2. Follow the student code of conduct.
  3. Work diligently and satisfactorily complete homework nightly.
  4. Accept responsibility for their behavior.
  5. Maintain a clean and safe campus.
  6. Participate in enriching activities and volunteer within their communities.
